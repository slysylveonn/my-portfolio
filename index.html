About Me





Sophie is currently enrolled at Humber College, studying for a diploma in Computer Programming.
She currently resides in Etobicoke, Ontario with her husband, dogs, cats and aquarium.
Despite her busy household, she is dedicating as much time as possible to her studies and nurturing her passions for all things IT, coding and website design related!
Sophie is a long-time fan of video games, starting her electronic adventures as a little girl playing Pokemon, Super Smash Bros and Sonic the Hedgehog.
As a proud adult gamer, she is currently invested in Final Fantasy and Stardew Valley, among others.
Sophie hopes to extend her knowledge of programming and design to the video game world one day so she can bring her lifelong passion into a real, saleable career.

Thank you for visiting my website! I would love to chat about any possible work and/or learning opportunities with you.
